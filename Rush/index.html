About Chimp Rush

Chimp Rush is an exciting VR multiplayer game where players take control of agile chimps, racing through diverse environments and using parkour-like moves to overcome obstacles. Compete against friends or online players in fast-paced, adrenaline-filled matches.

Features

  • Fast-paced parkour-style movement
  • Immersive VR gameplay
  • Multiplayer mode with friends or global players
  • Diverse maps: urban, industrial, abstract
  • Customizable characters
